Registration Questions

When does registration close?
Registration closes on March 1st, 2026, or when tickets sell out.
Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?
Yes, ticket transfers are allowed up to 48 hours before the event. Please email support.
Is there a student discount?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for students with a valid ID.

Event Details

What is the dress code?
The dress code is casual / business casual. Wear what makes you comfortable!
Will meals be provided?
Yes, breakfast, lunch, and snacks are provided on all conference days.
Is the venue wheelchair accessible?
Absolutely. The entire venue is fully accessible.

Accommodation

Do you have partner hotels?
Yes, we have discounted rates with nearby hotels. Check your confirmation email for details.
Is parking available?
Yes, free parking is available for all attendees at the venue.

Technical Requirements

Do I need to bring a laptop?
It is recommended for workshops, but not required for keynote sessions.
Is there Wi-Fi?
Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the venue.

Networking

Is there a networking party?
Yes, the official after-party is on the evening of Day 1 (March 15).
Is there an app for attendees?
Yes, the DevConf 2026 app will be available in February for networking.
